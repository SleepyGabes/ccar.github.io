Pets change our lives. We're on a quest to change theirs.

Quest for Compassionate Critters Animal Rescue is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing, fostering, and rehoming the abandoned, sick, and injured animals around our city. With you help, we hope to make a difference in the lives of these wonderful animals.

How We Help

Animal Rescue

We go on quests to rescue and recover any dog or cat roaming our streets.

Fostering and Adoption

We help our rescues find their temporary and forever homes.

Veterinary Services

We provide free veterinary treatment to strays, rescues, and pets.
